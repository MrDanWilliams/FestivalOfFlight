Frequently Asked Questions

Open Doors

What time should I arrive?

Doors open at 10am, please bring your ticket with you and allow yourself time to park beforehand.

Is free Wi-Fi available?

Sorry, guest Wi-Fi is not available.

Where can I park?

Free parking is available on campus. When you arrive, please follow the signage to 'Open Doors parking'.

Is there an age limit?

There are no ID requirements or age limits to enter the event but children aged under 16 must be accompanied by an adult with a valid ticket. There may be age restrictions on some activities.

Can I bring food?

Yes, you can bring food but alcohol is strictly prohibited. There will also be outlets available on site to purchase food and light refreshments.

Can I bring my dog or other pets?

Unfortunately animals are not permitted. This is for health and safety reasons and to limit distress to animals. Please also note that stewards will patrol the car parks and action will be taken if an animal has been left in a car.

How do I book a ticket?

Unfortunately, the Open Doors event has now sold out.

Can I buy tickets on the day?

Tickets cannot be purchased at the gate but you can buy tickets online on the day.

Are there any ticket concessions?

Each adult ticket includes one free child ticket (aged between three and 16 inclusive).

Children aged between three and 16 inclusive (accompanied by an adult holding a valid adult ticket) can buy a ticket at the child price of £5

Infants under three may enter free of charge.

What happens if the event is cancelled?

In the unlikely event we have to cancel Open Doors, the Air Display, or both, we will do all we can to notify you as soon as we have this information confirmed – via our website and all our social media channels.

How can I travel to the show?

If you are unable to drive to the show, the public transport services below are also available.

Rail
From London St Pancras to Bedford. A bus service is available from Bedford to Cranfield University (see ‘Bus Travel’ below).
London Euston to Milton Keynes Central. Frequent, fast services. A bus service is available from Milton Keynes station to Cranfield University (see ‘Bus Travel’ below)
The National Rail website gives timetable information for all train operating companies.
Their phone number is +44 (0)8457 48 49 50.


Bus
Different companies refer to the University in different ways. The main bus stop is named either as 'Cranfield University' or 'Wharley End (Cranfield), Cranfield University main gate'.

Route planning information is available at:
Traveline SouthEast
Google Transit

Buses to and from Bedford:
Cranfield Connect – Route C1
Stagecoacht – Route 2

Buses to and from Milton Keynes:
Cranfield Connect – Route C1

Coaches from major UK airports:
National Express – coaches run from several major UK airports to nearby towns.

If you should have ant questions regarding the above bus services please contact the relevant company direct via their website


Taxi
There are several taxi companies operating from Milton Keynes, Bedford or locally at Cranfield. The taxi journey from Milton Keynes or Bedford takes about 20 minutes to Cranfield.

Is parking on hard standing (concrete) or grass?

Parking is both on grass and hard standing.

Can I return to my car during the day?

Yes, you are free to go back and forth to your car during the day through our security gate. Remember to let us know whether you have a disability and we will provide you with a parking space closer to the action.

Can I bring my own food/picnic/foldaway chairs?

Yes, we welcome and encourage you to bring along food, picnics and foldaway chairs. There will also be catering concessions on-site where you can purchase food and light refreshments but please remember - do not bring alcohol. Alcohol is prohibited and will not be sold on-site.

Can I have a barbeque on site?

No, barbeques are not allowed on site.

Air Display

How much does it cost?

An Adult Ticket is £12 including booking fees. Each Adult ticket includes one free Child Ticket*
Any additional Child Tickets are £5 including booking fees. Children must be accompanied by an adult with a valid Adult Ticket.
* Child Ticket - young people aged between 3-16 inclusive
** Infants under 3 may enter free of charge
