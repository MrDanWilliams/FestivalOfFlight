About Flying Start, the global charity partnership with British Airways and Comic Relief

With support from customers, suppliers and colleagues todate we have raised 13.4 million since the partnership began in June 2010.

Flying Start will create lasting change by giving children a brighter future; whether that is providing a break from caring for sick family members, the opportunity to work in safer conditions or the chance to break free from the grip of poverty of living on the streets.

Flying Start combines Comic Relief's expertise in tackling some of the causes of the world's poverty, with British Airways long-standing commitment to community investment in the countries in which it operates.

Facts

  • Since 1985 Comic Relief has funded over 18,000 projects in the UK, who have helped millions of people leading incredibly tough lives.
  • Comic Relief has helped over 46.4 million people living in some of the world's poorest communities (outside the UK).
  • Comic Relief has helped over 11.5 million people leading incredibly tough lives across the UK.
  • Since Comic Relief began, it has helped more than 1.2 million people into education, through both formal and informal opportunities, across the world's poorest Communities.
  • BA target to raise 20 million by 2020
