Open Doors

Sold Out

Cranfield University’s first Open Doors event in more than a decade will give you the chance to look behind the scenes.

Tours of campus will explore some of our most innovative technology; showing a variety of our research from autonomous vehicles to sewage systems.

You can discover our past with our heritage display and look into the future with students and academics sharing their research in street theatre style.

And our Exploratorium brings research to life for the children, where they can play with robots, make paper aeroplanes, rockets and clay models... all in the name of science.


10:00 – 13:00 Campus Tours

Why not book onto a campus tour? You can see behind the scenes of the Formula 1 world in our Impact Centre, view laser marking and a demonstration of our virtual reality experience. You can register on the day. Places are limited and will go quickly, so make sure you arrive early.

10:00 – 13:00 Exploratorium

Children can get their hands dirty and take part in family-friendly experiments and activities in the Exploratorium. They can find mini beasts in soil, make and launch rockets and use their body and hand movements to control a robot.

10:00 – 13:00 Heritage Exhibition

This interactive exhibition will give you a fascinating insight into Cranfield’s 70-year history, as well as a look to our exciting future. Find out more about the people who have helped Cranfield to build its global reputation.

10:00 – 13:00 Vehicle Display

A whole host of cars will be on show, from classic to modern to veteran and military.
